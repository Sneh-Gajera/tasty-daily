The potato, Solanum tuberosum, is a starchy, underground tuberous crop cultivated worldwide. Its versatility in cooking makes it a dietary staple. Potatoes vary in size, shape, and color, with white, yellow, and red varieties. Rich in carbohydrates, potassium, and vitamin C, they offer sustenance and flavor. Common cooking methods include boiling, frying, mashing, and baking. Potatoes contribute to numerous culinary dishes, from fries and chips to mashed potatoes and stews.
